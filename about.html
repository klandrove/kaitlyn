About Me

I was born and raised in South Florida, proudly embracing my Cuban descent, including the language and traditions passed down by my grandparents. I am in my final semester at the University of Miami, where I will graduate in May with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Interactive Media.

I am passionate about creating unique and accessible user experiences on the web, whether from scratch in an empty HTML file or using frameworks such as Bootstrap. I have knowledge of full-stack development, a variety of data analytic tools, quality assurance principles, and scrum methodologies.

As the Vice President of Marketing for Kappa Theta Pi Sigma Chapter, a professional technology fraternity, I frequently collaborate with diverse individuals who share a passion for technology. When not experimenting with Figma or Adobe Creative Cloud products, you can find me at sporting events, especially baseball and hockey games. Sometimes, you may even find me on the field as a ball girl!

I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges, striving to blend creativity with technical skills to build engaging digital experiences.
